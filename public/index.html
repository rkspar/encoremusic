Encore Music Academy

The Academy

Our instructors are professional musicians with college degrees in music performance and education who are dedicated to encouraging your child and developing their creativity through music.

They are led by Encore Music Academy Director and founder, Debora Sanchez, a professionally trained pianist who has been teaching piano for more than 14 years, beginner to advanced as well as college level students, and is guided by her passion for developing the musical talents and abilities of students.

Sanchez graduated Cum Laude in the spring of 2003 from Florida International University with a double major in Piano Performance and Music Education and received an award for outstanding academic achievement in Music. "Word of mouth" is the key to our success thanks to the wonderful faculty and EMA's positive and professional atmosphere at all times!

Our Mission

Encore Music Academy's mission is to nurture and develop the musical talent of every student. At EMA students will be able to grow musically in a creative, professional and positive atmosphere. Our faculty of professional musicians and highly qualified teachers believes in teaching the art of music in a constructive and encouraging way.

Students of all ages will enjoy private lessons in their chosen instrument and will have the opportunity to develop a deep appreciation of music and the arts, while learning to play an instrument, study music theory, solfege, composition, musical production, sound recording and music history. Our comprehensive music curriculum is designed for the student that wants to learn music as an extra-curricular activity as well as for those students that want to become professional musicians.

The positive, professional and friendly atmosphere at encore music academy makes the learning process a very pleasant one!


Curriculum

Encore Music Academy offers private (one to one) lessons from the beginner to the advanced level in a variety of instruments as well as group lessons in music theory, solfege, musical production, sound recording, music history, jazz ensemble & keyboard band. The group lessons help students understand the fundamentals of music in an interactive and fun way while making the private lessons more focused on technique and performance skills of the chosen instrument. Students may enroll in private lessons only or in a combination of private lessons and group lessons. Encore Music Academy accepts students all year round (children and adults).

The recommended ages to start private lessons vary according to the instrument of choice. Encore Music Academy also offers Kindermusik group classes designed for students between the ages of 12 months and 4 years of age. These group classes are recommended for the really young children in order to stimulate and develop motor skills from early age.

Encore Music Academy also prepares students for auditions to performing arts magnet programs as well as college auditions to enter into university for a career in music. An important part of our curriculum is the exposure of our students in recitals, competitions and evaluations. Students from EMA will participate in at least two EMA recitals (one in December at Florida International University and another one in June at the University of Miami Victor E. Clarke Recital Hall).

According to their level, each student will have the opportunity to participate in local competitions and evaluations that will provide extra performance opportunities. Recital, evaluations outside Encore Music Academy and competitions are subject to extra fees. A list of all registrations fees is available before enrolling students into these events. Encore Music Academy also offers Masterclasses with world renowned musicians so that students can broaden their musical knowledge to even higher levels.

JOSE ANGEL NAVARRO
Guitar

Using his technical expertise in the guitar, Mr. Jose Angel Navarro has been able to bring to the concert halls throughout the world the sounds and the concepts of Afro-Cuban music. His compositions on the Afro concept have been honored and celebrated by the masters of the instrument as being a total innovation in the sense of conceptual sonority and sound as a patrimony inside the composition for the guitar of Latin America.

His personally developed technique is acclaimed by music’s best as being unique in the world. Included in this technique, is his ability to imitate the sounds of the traditional ceremonial drums on the guitar strings by applying a left-handed muffled harmonics with simultaneous played notes. While he is classified by the guitar world as being one of the fastest guitar players in the world, he has an ability to make his hands seem to be at a standstill.
